Blue Mocha Latte

Description

I am obsessed with matcha, but sensitive to caffeine. Lately I have been addicted to an afternoon beverage. This is where blue matcha comes in handy, it is caffeine free. This blue matcha latte (vegan) is a great way to enjoy a caffeine free latte. This latte is ridiculously easy to make. It only takes 5 minutes to make. All you need are 3 ingredients and a way to heat water.

Why you will Love this recipe

  1. It is gluten free, dairy free, egg free and plant based.
  2. Thanks to the butterfly pea powder it is caffeine free.
  3. It is easy to make and ready in less than 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • blue matcha powder
  • Vegan Milk
  • maple syrup

Steps

  1. Add blue matcha, maple syrup and hot water to a cup and mix.
  2. Add milk to latte
